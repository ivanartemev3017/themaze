Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Maze (the "Game") handles information when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

The Maze is a single-player offline game. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, location, or payment details.

The Game may collect anonymous, non-identifiable technical data (such as crash reports and performance metrics) through Unity's built-in services to help us improve stability. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. Third-Party Services

The Game is built with Unity. Unity may collect anonymous diagnostic and analytics data in accordance with their own privacy policy:

  • Unity Privacy Policy

No advertising SDKs, social media SDKs, or other third-party tracking libraries are included in the Game.

3. Data Storage

Any game progress (such as completed levels) is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to any server.

4. Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. Since the Game collects no personal data at all, it is safe for use by players of all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
